Project 1

Overview

For project 1 I will be designing a business website for Emma Lynne Photography. This site will showcase the work that Emma does, allow potential clients to see pricing and scheduling information, and contact Emma to begin arranging for photo shoots. The intended user for this website is a potential client.

Wireframe


Site Map


Home Page

  1. Page Name: Home
  2. Purpose: This page will serve as the main landing page for the site and will have snippets of the other content on the site.
  3. Audience: The audience for this page will be potential customers; it will give them a brief overview of Emma's services
  4. Content: This page will have the site header and footer, as well as a page-width cover image near the top with Emma's logo (or "Emma Lynne Photography" in a fancy font) centered on it. Further down the page, there will be an exerpt from the about me page with a link to that page, and a 2-3 photos total from the portrait and equestrian photo galleries.
  5. Data entry: None.
  6. Field validation: Not applicable.
  7. Buttons/hyperlinks/dropdowns: The shortened "about me" blurb will have a link to the about me page, and the equestrian and portrait photos will have links to the full galleries.
  8. Actions on page: The only actions that will take place on this page will be links to other pages.

About Me

  1. Page name: About Me
  2. Purpose: This page will have information about Emma and her photography background, and some brief personal background to make the site feel relatable.
  3. Audience: The audience for this page will be potential customers; it will allow Emma a chance to give the reader a sense of her personality.
  4. Content: This page will contain the site header and footer, as well as content about Emma, her personal background, and her photography background. There will be pictures interspersed for style.
  5. Data entry: Not applicable.
  6. Field validation: Not applicable.
  7. Buttons/hyperlinks/dropdowns: This page may include minimal links; any featured photos will link to their corresponding gallery, for example.
  8. Actions on page: The only actions on this page will be linking to other pages on the site.

Services/Pricing Page

  1. Page name: Services & Pricing
  2. Purpose: The purpose of this page will be to inform potential customers of the services that Emma offers, and her base pricing.
  3. Audience: The audience for this page is potential and/or current customers.
  4. Content: This page will have sections for each service that Emma offers containing details on pricing and what is included in that service. There will also be example images and links to the appropriate galleries.
  5. Data entry: None
  6. Field validation: Not applicable.
  7. Buttons/hyperlinks/dropdowns: This page will contain the standard header and footer, and links to other pages on the site, like the gallery pages.
  8. Actions on page: The only actions on this page will be links to other pages, such as the galleries.

Scheduling Page

  1. Page name: Scheduling
  2. Purpose: The purpose of this page will be to allow users to schedule a photo shoot or to inquire about doing so.
  3. Audience: The audience for this page is customers and potential customers.
  4. Content: This page will contain the standard header and footer, as well as some type of contact applet (for customers to contact Emma) or a scheduling applet. This will require integration of an outside resource.
  5. Data entry: This page will require input of information like name, phone number/email, and other information required for scheduling.
  6. Field validation: Data entry will use HTML input validation for things like emails, phone numbers, and quantity of photos desired.
  7. Buttons/hyperlinks/dropdowns: This page will contain a few buttons as required for using the scheduling/contact applets.
  8. Actions on page: The actions possible on this page will be interaction with whatever outside services allow for contacting Emma or scheduling a photoshoot (likely something like Google Forms or Google Calendar).

Equestrian Photo Gallery

  1. Page name: Equestrian Gallery
  2. Purpose: This page will show off Emma's equestrian photography so that customers can see what type of photos they can expect.
  3. Audience: Customers and potential customers.
  4. Content: This page will contain a gallery of sample equestrian photos that the user can cycle through.
  5. Data entry: None.
  6. Field validation: Not applicable.
  7. Buttons/hyperlinks/dropdowns: There will be buttons to select a photo and go to the previous and next photo in the gallery.
  8. Actions on page: The actions on the page will include selecting a photo as well as moving to the next or previous photo in the gallery.

Portrait Photo Gallery

  1. Page name: Portrait Gallery
  2. Purpose: This page will exhibit Emma's portrait photography so that customers can see what type of photos they can expect.
  3. Audience: The audience for this page will be potential and current customers.
  4. Content: This page will contain a photo gallery of sample portraits that the user can cycle through.
  5. Data entry: None.
  6. Field validation: Not applicable
  7. Buttons/hyperlinks/dropdowns: This page will include buttons for selecting the image you want to view as well as cycling to the next or previous image.
  8. Actions on page: The actions on this page will include selecting a photo as well as moving to the next or previous photo in the gallery.
