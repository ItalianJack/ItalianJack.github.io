Welcome

Hey there! I'm Jack, a private pilot, Civil Air Patrol cadet and aviation enthusiast. Here I'll share the story of how I became a private pilot and everything I love about flying.

Part 1: In the beginning...

I've never been too far from airplanes growing up. My dad became a private pilot while he was in the service, and even though he didn't fly for work it was not unusual for us to have fighter jets flying over our house. We'd go to airshows together, and he even knew a pilot or two from the Thunderbirds. Suffice it to say, anytime we were around an airport I was having a good time. Though I wasn't the type to fantasize about becoming a pilot, I certainly thought it would be cool to do so. Little did I know what was in store for me in the future.
